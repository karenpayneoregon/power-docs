# Agent flows overview


Agent flows are the classic automation experience in Copilot Studio. You create and manage them in the classic experience.

Flows in Copilot Studio automate repetitive tasks and integrate your apps and services. You can trigger a flow manually, based on other automated events or agents, or on a schedule.
